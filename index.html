From Aspiring Mechanic to Award-Winning Videographer

Passion for cars meets professional content creation

My journey into automotive videography started in 2022 when I discovered my true passion wasn't just working on cars - it was capturing their essence and energy in motion. What began as an aspiring mechanic's hobby quickly evolved into a deep love for automotive storytelling.

By 2024, I took the leap to go professional with ALX.Edits, combining my mechanical understanding of vehicles with high-energy visual techniques. This unique perspective allows me to showcase cars in ways that resonate with both enthusiasts and buyers - helping dealerships move inventory and events attract crowds.

Based in Hamilton and serving all of New Zealand, I've built a reputation for fast turnaround without sacrificing quality. From same-day event coverage to cinematic brand films, my work has generated over 10 million views for clients across NZ and Australia.
